We invite your proposals for events to shape 2018 Indianapolis Design Week. Indy Design Week is comprised of diverse events dedicated to exploring the ways design informs the past, present, and future tenses of our lives in the city. Upon acceptance of your proposal, the Indianapolis Design Week steering committee will promote the event that you agree to organize.


Indy Design Week is a singular opportunity to stitch together the spaces between the over 361 square miles that make up Indianapolis. Our goal is to ignite relationships, envision a bolder Indy, and engage the design profession to participate in shaping the city's present and future.
